Overview

The Space Debris Tracking company is based in San Francisco. The founders, long time stargazers obsessed with efficiency and patterns, have come together to create a company that can help manage space debris and ensure the safety of space missions. Our mission is to provide high-quality tracking services and assessments of collision risks for all space debris, regardless of size or orbit, and to recommend necessary actions to avoid any potential risks.
